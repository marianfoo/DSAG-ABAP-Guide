## The new DSAG ABAP guide

Welcome to the new “ABAP Guide” from DSAG. Before you is a comprehensive document on the topic of application development in SAP.

As standard software, the SAP Business Suite is characterized by a high degree of flexibility and expandability. Customer-specific adjustments and extensions can be found in almost all companies that use SAP software. The SAP software is therefore subject to continuous adaptation and expansion on both the manufacturer and customer sides due to changing customer needs.
The high degree of flexibility and expandability of SAP software brings with it advantages and disadvantages:

* The software can be optimally adapted to customer-specific requirements and thus the added value can be significantly increased through its use.  
* At the same time, expandability entails the risk of customer-specific developments that are complex, difficult to maintain and prone to errors.

The previous versions of the ABAP guide were published in 2012 and 2016. Application development in SAP has changed significantly since then and has become significantly more complex. While most previously development was done in ABAP and GUI-based tools, today it is necessary to use and master various technologies and tools. Since then, the following significant and major innovations have found their way into application development with SAP:  

* The introduction of the ABAP Development Tools (ADT)
* HANA Datenbank
* S/4HANA Business Suite
* CDS for data modeling
* the Restful Application Programming Model (RAP)  
* and the rapid development of the ABAP programming language to ABAP-Cloud

to name the most important innovations here.  
The SAP community was therefore called upon to provide an updated guide that takes into account the innovations in the SAP development.  
In 2024, in response to the DSAG's call, a team of experts in the SAP environment came together again to create a new version of the ABAP guide, which provides both those responsible in companies as well as developers and consultants with orientation, hints and tips from practice and supports the successful and targeted use of proven and new technologies for application development.

## Structure and contents of the guide
  
The success factors of the transformation to modern ABAP development are not primarily found in the technical topics, but are created by the framework conditions that a development team finds. At the beginning of the guide you will receive explanations and recommendations regarding the **development organization**, which will give you guidance on how to create a framework for modern application development in SAP.  
You will then receive an overview of the concepts of **"Clean Core"** and recommendations on how to proceed. Understanding the Clean Core concept is important so that you can define your strategies and also understand how the new development methodologies bring the Clean-Core approach into implementation. This gives you a good overview of the general conditions.  

The topics 

* **Core Data Services**
* **ABAP**
* **ABAP Unit Test**  
* **User Interfaces and**
* **Forms**

give you explanations, recommendations and details on the technology-oriented aspects and technologies of modern SAP development.  
At the beginning of each chapter you will find introductory information and an overview of the topic. This part is primarily intended for managers and decision-makers in the company who want to get an overview of the area.  
The deeper you go into the chapter, the more detailed information you will receive on the individual topics. These sections are particularly suitable for architects, developers and those interested in technology who want to gain a more detailed insight into the topic.  

In the further part you will find sections on the topics of **Open Source** with important explanations as to why open source is also important in the ABAP area, explanations on Application Lifecycle Management (ALM), where you will also find explanations of modern methods of **version management**.  
Finally, you will find information on the topics of **security** and **integration**, which are more important than ever when developing SAP software.
And of course, references to artificial intelligence should not be missing here.

## Positioning

The SAP has numerous documentation on application development and expansion of the SAP platform. Specialist publishers have also published very good publications on this subject. Furthermore, there are now numerous freely available learning journeys on various topics. However, it is not easy to find your way through the jungle of tools and techniques.  
The added value of this document lies in the summary of best practices, practical tips and tried-and-tested regulations from the user companies. Therefore, this guide primarily addresses the topics from a slightly higher perspective.

This guideline is intended to give you, as a user, developer, development, project or IT manager, suggestions and support so that you don't have to keep reinventing the wheel and can build on the experiences of others. The recommendations presented in this guideline do not claim to be complete or absolutely valid, but rather represent a selection of practical tips.

As a team of authors, we tried to find the right mix between overview knowledge and depth of detail. We therefore refer to further sources or literature worth reading at appropriate points in order not to redundantly reproduce topics that have already been discussed in detail.  

## Motivation

Some of the newer technologies and tools are not yet in full use, even though they have been available for years. It is therefore important to the team of authors to demonstrate, based on advantages and possibilities, why it is worth investing in the new methods and exploring new paths in application development.  
This guide is intended to enable you to assess the status of SAP development in the company and show areas of action on how you can achieve efficient and good SAP development in the company.  
To do this, the framework conditions in the SAP development environment must be adapted or even changed. The ABAP guide is intended to help you break down inhibitions, make it easier to get started with the topics and motivate you to actively challenge and promote the topics described in the guide.
