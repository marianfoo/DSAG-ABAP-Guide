1. TOC

## A management-friendly summary  
The use of SAP standard processes and expansion options offers companies a future-proof, maintainable and agile basis for innovation, digitalization and transformation. With a clear focus on standard solutions, you minimize technical debt, promote efficient upgrades and are optimally prepared for the challenges of the Cloud era. Always consider when to build custom extensions and when to use another platform for the extension or business process. 

## Reasons for using SAP standard processes and expansion options  

### Future security and innovation  
SAP standard transactions and applications are improved and expanded with each S/4HANA release. SAP offers new innovations exclusively on standard processes, especially in the public Cloud. Using these processes ensures that companies can benefit from future updates and developments. Development based on standard APIs and thus standard data products is future-proof.   

### Reduction of technical debt  
 Reducing technical debt makes it easier to adopt audit/compliance-compliant and security-by-default techniques. Example: Custom APIs based on RAP in the managed scenario enable a secure data update process. In the long term, the effort for maintenance and adjustments is reduced, which supports an agile and secure IT landscape. 
 Either with an inventory or with each new requirement, you can perform an assessment of your custom code using the 4R approach: Reuse, Retire, Refactor and Replatform. By defining your Clean Core policies, you can actively refactor (Get Clean phase) through projects. After or before that, all new developments should be carried out according to the guidelines (Stay Clean phase). 

### Improve upgrade ability  
A big advantage of the Clean Core concept is the possibility of quick and smooth upgrades. Standard processes enable upgrades without extensive adjustments and testing. This means companies can benefit more quickly from new technologies and SAP innovations.  

### Promoting digitalization and innovation  
Please note two aspects for future business process improvements:
* Seamlessly leverage SAP innovations: By leveraging the SAP standard, companies can seamlessly integrate new technologies and integrate innovations into their business processes.  
* Digitization of business processes: Standard solutions, such as standard Fiori apps, can be expanded to include artificial intelligence in SAP BTP and form the basis for consistent digitization and automation.  

### Efficient use and expansion of SAP solutions  
 SAP standard solutions and interfaces (e.g. Business Accelerator Hub) enable the use of a single source rather than maintaining multiple isolated non-standard solutions. The adaptations are based on the proven SAP expansion mechanisms, which promote a future-proof architecture and will continue to be expanded and used by SAP in the future.  

### Preparing for the Cloud transformation  
Through the Clean Core concept and the use of SAP standard transactions and applications, companies can move their business processes to the Cloud as soon as it makes sense. With SAP tools, but also with non-SAP systems and platforms, the transformation can be designed flexibly and according to needs.  

### Easy integration of third-party tools  
Don't reinvent the wheel, especially with ABAP legacy tools. Evaluate other solutions, including third-party ones, see [SAP Store](https://www.sap.com/germany/store.html). Remember that the SAP standard processes and interfaces simplify the integration of third-party solutions so that companies can quickly expand their IT landscape.

### Cost-effectiveness

Is switching to Clean Core even economical for your company? Some solutions have been developed over decades and bring great added value to the company. A switch to Clean Core means a high investment and sometimes a completely new construction of these solutions.

### Know How

Do you already have the necessary know-how for the transformation in your company? The development, but also the consulting, requires a lot of new knowledge for current technologies and best practices on a S/4HANA system in order to switch to Clean Core.
